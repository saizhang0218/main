About

I am a PhD student of Computer Science at Harvard Uniersity. Before coming to Harvard, I received my bachelor degree and master degree in Electrical Engineering from University of Toronto. My main research interest lies in accelerating deep learning computations on modern hardware platforms. Additionally, I also have a broadly interest in designing deep learning systems and multi-agent reinforcement learning.
